Wire Abilities

Almost all wires have an ability associated to them. An ability is activated when a spark is place on the wire that is connected to the wire.

Build a Card

Pick a card from the supply and add it to the board. A card has to be connected to to another card's edge so that the gray wires are connected. You can pick the rotation of the card on placement. If there are no cards in the supply, take the top card of the draw pile.


Rotate a Card

Pick the orientation of a card on the board. You cannot rotate the card with a spark on it.

Gain a Victory Point

Add a point to your VP counter. You still place a point cube on the point slot.

Gain an Energy Token

Collect an energy token. This can only be used this turn.

Claim a Memory Slot

When passing a memory, place one of your player markers on top of the memory Slot. If there is a different player marker already on the Memory Slot replace it. Point tokens are not placed on the Memory Slot and player markers are not removed unless being replaced.

Claim a Capictor

The first time a Capictor is passed, take one of your player markers and place it over the Capictor Slot. Any future sparks pass the wire node with that Capictor, will place a point token on top of the capictor and player marker. The player marker is not removed and stays till the end of the game.
