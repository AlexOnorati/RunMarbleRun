Setup

Place the starter circuit card (Card 001) in the middle of the play area with side A up. The board will be build out from this card so make plenty sure there is plenty of space. Shuffle circuit cards 002-050, Place them face down as a draw pile then draw 8 circuit cards face down and place them around the starter card. Forming a 3x3 grid with the starter card in the middle. Rotate the cards in the grid randomly and flip over all cards in the grid. When there are any Power Cards place a power token on the card assoicated to the card. Set point tokens, and circuit cards (Draw pile)to the side. Draw cards until there are cards equal players plus one in the supply area. All players then pick a color and collect the matching spark, player markers and a victory point dial. Randomly select a Level 1, Level 2 and Level 3 event card. Place them facedown in a stack with Level 1 on top, Level 2 middle and Level 3 on bottom. Do not reveal any of the events.

First Time Play

For your first game we recommend these setup alterations.

On Step use cards 002-009

On Step Use event cards R1:E3, R2:E3, and R3:E2.
