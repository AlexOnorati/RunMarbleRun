Alternate Play

Once you have played a few times, we recommend doing some alternate play! You can mix and match all alternate modes.

Triangle Gate

Add cards 051-070

When a Triangle Gate card is built, place a Triangle gate. ()

A spark will move to the open wire the gate is not blocking. The spark will go straight if the wire is fully blocked by the gate. (Indicated by the arrow)

Once the spark has past the gate, the gate's position is toggled by flipping the gate. A battery () can be placed on the battery slot to toggle the gate. The gate will still toggle after the spark has passed.

Directional Pad

Add cards 071-080

When a Directional card is built, place a Directional Pad () on the card. A spark will move straight through the card. A battery () can be placed on the battery slot to pick the direction of the spark will go, including the way the spark came from!

Springs

Add cards 081-100 also add event card R3:E3 for setup.

Some rotational gate and directional pad cards will now have the symbol. When a spark passes this icon, it will skip the adjacent circuit card and continue onto the next card in that direction. This can be used to skip a max a gab of one. When a spark travels over a spring and does not land back on the board it has considered to loose it's charge.
